About Me

I love to create, and I love to learn. What draws me to programming is that mix of creativity and technology. I've been interested in tech forever - I was that kid playing with the Commodore 64, copying BASIC code from a magazine at lunchtime.

While I've worked at large corporations, I also have an entrepreneurial spirit, having helped grow an online e-learning business to over 100,000 students in 190+ countries.

I joined the Coder Factory Academy Fast Track program in 2017 because I wanted to learn real-world programming skills.
